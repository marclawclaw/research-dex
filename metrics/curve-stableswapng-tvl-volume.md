## Capital efficiency highlight

| Metric | Value | Period | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|--------|
| USDC/USDT pool volume-to-TVL ratio | ~50x | Early 2025 | [Curve Week 8 2025](https://news.curve.finance/curve-best-yields-key-metrics-week-8-2025/) |

This extremely high volume-to-TVL ratio (50x) demonstrates the capital efficiency unlocked by StableSwapNG's dynamic fee pools. The USDC/USDT pool (Curve.fi USD Strategic Reserve) consistently generated nearly 50 times more volume than its locked capital, driven by aggregator routing and the low-slippage stableswap curve.

## Historical context

Curve Finance's aggregate TVL peaked near $24B at end of 2022 (Curve V1/V2 era). Post-FTX and the DeFi bear market, TVL contracted to approximately $2B to $2.5B through 2023 to 2024. StableSwapNG launched in mid-2023 and has become the dominant pool factory for new pool deployments. The 2025 recovery in fee share (1.6% to 44% of Ethereum DEX fees) is attributed largely to the dynamic fee feature introduced in StableSwapNG pools.
